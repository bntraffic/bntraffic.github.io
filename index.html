Bloomington Normal Traffic

About

As a group consisting of members passionate about computer science, we have decided to compile our talents to make our community a safer place. It is our goal to make roads safer for everyone in the city of Bloomington-Normal. To accomplish this, we have gathered data from various credible sources to design an interactive map that will encompass all the dangers presented by traffic to inform locals about our roads.

Project

We have designed a map to signify the high and low areas of traffic to give citizens of Bloomington Normal an idea of what roads are safest to drive on.
